Video Script: NFC vs Tap & Pay vs UPI (Telugu) Intro: Meeru eppudaina chusara… konni mandi phone ni just machine meeda tap chesi payment complete chestaru? QR scan kuda ledu… PIN kuda ledu… just tap and done! Idi ela work avutundi? UPI tho difference enti? Mana phone lo unda leda? Ivanni ee video lo telusukundam. NFC ante enti? NFC ante Near Field Communication. Idi oka wireless technology. Rendu devices chala daggara unte matrame communication jarugutundi. Example: phone to payment machine, metro cards, access cards. Simple ga: NFC ante technology. Tap & Pay ante enti? Tap & Pay ante NFC use chesi payments cheyyadam. Phone unlock chesi machine meeda tap chesthe payment ayipothundi. So: NFC = Technology Tap & Pay = Payment feature Tap & Pay vs UPI: UPI: QR scan or UPI ID Internet kavali Konchem time padutundi Tap & Pay: Scan avasaram ledu Just tap Fast payment Simple: UPI = Scan & Pay Tap & Pay = Just Tap & Pay Anni mobiles lo NFC untunda? NFC anni phones lo undadu. Mostly mid-range and premium phones lo untundi. Check: Settings → NFC search. Advantages: Fast Easy No QR needed Secure Contactless Disadvantages: All phones lo undadu All shops lo support ledu Limits untayi Battery required Phone lost ayite risk Conclusion: NFC = Technology Tap & Pay = Feature UPI = Most popular in India Like, Share, Follow!
